FOOD FOR DOGS

  • DRY DOG FOOD
    1. Dry dog food usually contains of bagged nuts that contain 3-11% water.It makes up the vast majority of pet foods.
    2. Dry food is both convenient and typically inexpensive.
    3. Dry food processing is popular in the pet food industry.
    4. It is energy efficient,allows for large amounts of feed to be used,and is cost effective.
Advantages Disadvantages
Ease of storage and feeding Lower palatability when compared to wet food
Cost effective More likely to contain preservatives
  • WET DOG FOOD
    1. Wet or canned dog food usually is packaged in a solid or soft-sided container.
    2. Wet food contains roughly 60-78% water,which is significantly higher in moisture than dry or semi-moist food.
    3. Wet food will often be higher in protein or fat compared to dry food.
    4. Once cooked,this mixture can be directly placed into cans to form a loaf or it can be cut into "meaty" pieces for chunks.
Advantages Disadvantages
Increased palatability Linked to weight gain
Often higher in protein and fat Spoilage
easier to eat Expensive
